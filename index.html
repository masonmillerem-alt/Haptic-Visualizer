Haptic Visualizer

Turn sound into real vibration for your bHaptics suit.

What It Does

Haptic Visualizer listens to audio in real time and converts loud events into vibration feedback for your bHaptics devices.

  • 🎧 Real-time audio detection
  • 💥 Detects explosions and bass drops
  • 🦾 Compatible with bHaptics suits
  • 🎛 Custom vibration modes
  • 🌈 Rainbow animated interface
  • 📊 Audio visualizer

Supported Devices

bHaptics Supported Devices:

  • TactSuit Pro
  • TactSuit Air
  • Tactosy Arms
  • Tactosy Hands
  • Tactosy Feet
  • Tactal

How To Use

  1. Install bHaptics Player
  2. Start Haptic Visualizer
  3. Select your suit model
  4. Play audio or video
  5. Feel the vibration
